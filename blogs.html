Construction of a Diagnostic Center at the Nsawam Female Prison in Ghana

We have begun building a Diagnostic Center at the Nsawam Female Prison to provide inmates with access to essential healthcare services such as lab tests, disease screening, and maternal care. This project, made possible through generous donations and community support, aims to improve the well-being of women in custody and ensure they receive timely medical attention.

Construction of a Diagnostic Center at the Nsawam Female Prison in Ghana

The construction of the Diagnostic Center at the Nsawam Female Prison is progressing steadily, with major milestones already achieved. From laying the foundation to raising the main structure, the project has seen remarkable growth in just a short time.

Once completed, the center will provide inmates with access to vital healthcare services including routine checkups, lab testing, and preventive care helping to improve their overall health and well-being. We are excited to share each phase of this journey and look forward to its completion, which will bring lasting impact to the prison community.
