Testimony

Renewed, Healed, and Spirit-Filled

At 86, I thought my ministry journey was over, but joining HYM has brought a fresh move of God in my life. I received the baptism of the Holy Spirit, the School of Ministry has deepened my understanding of God’s Word, and I have found a loving family in Christ. Most wonderfully, the Lord healed me from years of chronic arthritis pain, and today I feel renewed, healthy, and full of purpose.

— Merlene (USA)

Testimony

From Coma to Complete Restoration

" My little niece, just 7 years old, was involved in a car accident and went into a coma for six days. I believed God could raise her because, since joining HYM, I had witnessed and heard of many being healed, delivered, and restored. On Sunday, June 22, I brought the request before the ministry, and the whole fellowship stood in prayer with me. To the glory of God, my niece came out of the coma the very next day! Today, she has been discharged from the hospital and is doing well. I thank God so much for this miracle, and I am grateful for HYM—a ministry that truly intercedes and a God who faithfully hears and answers."

— Alex (USA)

Testimony

From Pain to Purpose

"For four months, I endured excruciating pain that made it difficult to walk without an aid. In a moment of despondency, I joined the HYM afternoon fellowship, holding on to faith and refusing to give up. There, Jesus touched me and healed me completely. My long desire to actively participate in ministry has now been fulfilled. Today, I not only walk in health but also pray for others and witness them receive their healing. The spiritual gifts within me have been awakened, and I am restored and empowered for God’s service."

— Elizabeth (USA)

Testimony

Breakthrough at Last

"After losing my job, I searched tirelessly for nine months without success. Then, a dear friend invited me to the HYM in-person retreat on August 10, 2025. Right there during the retreat, I received a call from one of the companies I had applied to. I was interviewed shortly after, and within a week, I received my offer letter! I am overjoyed and deeply grateful to God for this breakthrough. Truly, He makes all things beautiful in His time."

— Kay (Ghana)

Testimony

Hope Restored, Doors Opened

"Relocating to Scotland to join my family was my greatest joy, but after two years of being unable to secure a job, my hopes and aspirations were almost dashed. Then I joined the HYM prayer services, and my faith was strengthened. Within just a month, I wrote and passed my health professional exams, and in the same month, I was offered a good job. I give all the glory to God for turning my situation around! "

— Courage (Scotland)

Testimony

From Grief to Joy

"God Restored Me ! I lost my first pregnancy and was filled with grief and sorrow. But through Rev. Hannah’s daily prayers with me, my faith was lifted. I held firmly to the words of prophecy, waged warfare with them, and trusted God to fulfil His Word. To the glory of God, precisely one year later, in the very same month I had experienced the loss, the Lord blessed us with a beautiful baby boy. What was once a season of mourning has now become a testimony of joy and restoration. Joining HYM has not only brought this miracle but has also deepened my knowledge and experience of God. Truly, He is faithful!"

— Mimi (Ghana)
