Welcome to Hannah Yawson Ministry

HYM believes in living out God's call to serve others. Through our various outreach programs, we strive to bring comfort, guidance, and tangible support to those who are often forgotten or overlooked.

Our Ministry Calling

We are delighted to welcome you to Hannah Yawson Ministries (HYM), a Christian NGO, a place where compassion meets action. We are the hands and feet of Christ, bringing hope, healing, and renewal to all we encounter. Our ministry is rooted in Jesus' call to serve the vulnerable and marginalized with love, dignity, and hope.

Through our ministries, we seek to bring God's light and healing to those in need, walking alongside them on their journey toward transformation.

Founded by Rev. Hannah Yawson, our ministry has grown from a humble beginning to become a beacon of hope in our community, touching thousands of lives through our various outreach programs and spiritual initiatives.

- Rev. Hannah Aba Yawson

About HYM

HYM is a non-profit Christian organization dedicated to showing God's love through spiritual and social services. Guided by the teachings of Matthew 25:40, we believe that each person is worthy of compassion, respect, and the fullness of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of hope, restoring dignity and transforming lives through the love of Christ.

Our Mission

HYM exists to reflect God's love and compassion by serving those who are vulnerable and marginalized. We are dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through spiritual guidance, practical support, and acts of service.

Our Leadership Team

Meet the dedicated individuals guiding Hannah Yawson Ministry

Reverend Hannah Aba Yawson – President

Reverend Hannah Aba Yawson is a dedicated leader with over 25 years of experience in social policy, development, and ministry. As the President of Hannah Yawson Ministry (HYM), she leads the organization's mission to uplift vulnerable individuals and communities and foster spiritual growth.

As an experienced development practitioner, dynamic inspirational speaker, and dedicated revivalist, Reverend Hannah has positively impacted countless lives through her ministry work. She is passionate about guiding individuals in deepening their relationship with God and supporting their journey towards holistic well-being.

With a background in trauma care, Rev. Yawson offers compassionate guidance to individuals and communities affected by hardship. Her dedication to social justice and women's empowerment was demonstrated during her service on the Discharge Board of Nsawam Female Prison in Ghana.

Dr. Gideon Hosu-Porbley – Director

Gideon Hosu-Porbley is a seasoned international development research and monitoring & evaluation practitioner with over 20 years of experience. He possesses enormous skills in strategy development, project planning, and management, with thematic expertise in migration, education, gender, HIV/AIDS, environment, and governance.

Gideon holds academic degrees in Geography and Resource Development, Migration Studies, Development Studies, and Public Policy. He has received Visiting Fellowship Awards at the Institute of Development Studies, University of Sussex, UK, and serves as a lecturer at Ashesi University, Ghana.

Dr. Christiana Adu-Takyi – Director

Dr. Christiana Adu-Takyi is a medical practitioner, a Paediatric Cardiologist who has special interest in children with congenital and acquired heart diseases. This she translates in her own personal ministry with a passion to see others especially children and young adults have a heart of total submission to the power of God.

She is involved in medical Christian outreaches to the primary and senior high schools.

Mr. Alfred Koduah – Director

Alfred Koduah is a dedicated leader driven by a passion for lasting social change. With a strong commitment to serving underserved communities, he provides support in education, healthcare, and financial aid.

As a seasoned minister of the gospel with over 12 years of experience in preaching, teaching, and Human Resource Management, Alfred combines organizational expertise with a deep commitment to guiding others in faith. His leadership is characterized by compassion, integrity, and a sense of purpose that reflects the love and grace of Jesus Christ.

Reverend Isaac Kwafo Yawson – Board Chairman

Reverend Isaac Yawson is a business administration expert and a social work professional. He has over 25 years of experience in academia, management consultancy, and counselling. His experience spans corporate restructuring and business process re-engineering.

He also provides consultations in church administration and finance. He is a lecturer at the university and offers spiritual support and counselling to students and the academic community.
